## Concentrate

To get the Concentrate achievement, you need to complete at least 10 consecutive plays of a single song without missing any beats. 

1. Select the song you wish to complete the achievement on. 

2. Ensure that your Beat Saber settings are optimized for the song, adjust difficulty levels if needed.

3. Start the song and make sure you hit every beat without fail.

4. If you miss a beat, your attempt will be invalidated and you must start over again. 

5. After you have completed 10 consecutive successful attempts, you will unlock the Concentrate achievement. 

Congratulations!
